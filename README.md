## 👋 About

I am a web developer with over four years of experience building production websites for U.S. agencies, SaaS companies, and e-commerce brands. WordPress is the platform I work in most, with a focus on Gutenberg, ACF, custom themes, and PHP template work. I am also comfortable across Shopify, Squarespace, Wix, ClickFunnels, BigCommerce, and Webflow when projects call for them. AI tools are a regular part of my workflow, including Claude Code, Cursor, Antigravity, and Google's AI stack, which help me move through builds, audits, and debugging more efficiently without sacrificing quality.

> **Currently:** leading WordPress work across a 500-site multisite at Snexus LLC, and building mobile apps as passion projects on the side using Antigravity, Firebase, and Google AI Studio.
